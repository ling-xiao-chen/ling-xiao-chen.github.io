I study nationalism, political culture, public opinion formation, and authoritarian politics. My current project examines how nationalism and popular culture become entwined in China’s entertainment sector: the state enlists celebrities to amplify nationalist cues, and audiences interpret these nationalist cues to draw boundaries of national belonging.
